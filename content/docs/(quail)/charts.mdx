## Creating Charts

You can create charts in Quail through multiple methods:

<Tabs items={["Charts Page", "Chat Interface", "SQL Editor"]}>
  <Tab value="Charts Page">
    <Steps>
      <Step>Navigate to the **Charts** section in the main menu</Step>
      <Step>Click **Create New Chart**</Step>
      <Step>Select your data source (connection and table/query)</Step>
      <Step>Choose a chart type</Step>
      <Step>Configure your chart settings</Step>
      <Step>Save your chart</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="Chat Interface">
    <Steps>
      <Step>
        In the Chat interface, ask a question that would benefit from
        visualization - Example: "Show me monthly sales for the last year" -
        Example: "What's the distribution of customer ages?"
      </Step>
      <Step>
        When Quail generates a chart in response, click **Save Chart**
      </Step>
      <Step>Enter a name and description for your chart</Step>
      <Step>Select where to save the chart (standalone or to a dashboard)</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab value="SQL Editor">
    <Steps>
      <Step>Write a SQL query in the SQL Editor</Step>
      <Step>Run the query to see the results</Step>
      <Step>Click the **Visualize** button</Step>
      <Step>Choose a chart type that fits your data</Step>
      <Step>Configure visualization settings</Step>
      <Step>Click **Save Chart**</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Customizing Your Charts

<Accordions>
  <Accordion title="Prompt Agent" id="customize-style">
    <Steps>
      <Step>Open the chart editor and navigate to the **Style** tab</Step>
      <Step>
        Use the available options to adjust: 
        - Colors and themes 
        - Fonts and labels 
        - Grid lines and backgrounds
      </Step>
      <Step>Save your changes to apply the new style</Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Save Chart for Manual Editing" id="save-chart-manual">
    <Steps>
      <Step>After creating or editing your chart, click **Save**</Step>
      <Step>
        Save the chart to enable manual editing of the JSX components using 
        Fumadocs components for further customization
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</Accordions>
